Orange Smoothies

A glorious orange-banana smoothie on a hot day

When you think of smoothies you don't usually think of oranges, but with this recipe it might be the only thing on your mind when you hear the magic word smoothie.

combining the smoothness of bananas and the fresh citrus taste of oranges this smoothie will sure brighten up your day.

Useful as a quick drink from a hot day or even a full meal of breakfast you will love this smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cold milk
  • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
  • 1 banana
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 (8 oz) container of vanilla yogurt
  • 4 ice cubes

Directions

  1. In a blender blend the milk, oranges, banana, salt, and yogurt for 1 minute
  2. Add the ice cubes and blend until its smooth
  3. pour out into your favorite glass, serve, and enjoy!
