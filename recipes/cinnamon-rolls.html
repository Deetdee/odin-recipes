Cinnamon Rolls

Batch of Cinnamon Rolls straight from the oven!

Sweet Cinnamon Rolls are one of my favorite desserts, Whenever I see a Cinnabon restaurant I always get a six pack to go. Originally from Sweden,

these circular delights are not only fantastic straight from the oven but also as leftover treats the next day. I have made this recipe plenty of times

and it should provide a baker's dozen in a large baking dish and is best served with cold milk.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of white granulated or sugar cane sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 packet of instant or active-dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 egg

Cinnamon Filling

  • 2/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Vanilla Glaze

  • 1 1/2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup (1/2) stick of unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons of milk

Steps for Dough

  1. mix the water and milk together and heat up in a microwave safe cup for 30-40 seconds
  2. add the water/milk mixture to a large bowl and add 1 teaspoon of your white sugar
  3. open and add the entire packet of yeast to the bowl and give a good stir with a whisk, let it sit for 10 minutes
  4. in a separate bowl mix your flour and salt together with a whisk
  5. Melt your 2 tbsp of butter and add it with the rest of your sugar to your yeast mixture and give it a good whisk
  6. crack and add the whole egg and give another stir
  7. while stirring (switch to a wooden spoon), add parts of the flour until you have only a small part left and its sticking to your spoon
  8. grab and start kneading the dough for about 8 minutes (fold and push down then rotate 90 degrees and repeat for kneading)
  9. Once the dough is ready spray the inside of a large bowl with cooking spray and place the dough inside covered with plastic wrap and a towel for 1 hr
  10. after 1 hr take the dough out and roll into a large thin rectangle with a rolling pin then start making the filling

Steps for Cinnamon Filling

  1. Mix the brown sugar, ground cinnamon, and vanilla extract in a bowl with a spoon
  2. using your softened butter (if not softened use the microwave for 10-15 seconds) spread across the dough with a brush or knife
  3. spread the cinnamon mixture across all of the dough and optionally add other toppings as you like, such as pecans
  4. once fully coated, start tightly rolling one end to the other until you have a nice tight roll
  5. divide and cut the roll up into pieces, just mark and cut it in half repeatedly but make sure to have at least 13 pieces
  6. place them all into a baking pan and cover in plastic wrap to let it rise for 30-45 minutes
  7. preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit/190 degrees celsius and take some melted butter and coat the cinnamon rolls with a brush
  8. put them in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown

Steps for Vanilla Glaze

  1. mix your powdered sugar, melted butter, and vanilla extract together in a small bowl
  2. add the milk a tablespoon at a time until it hits your desired consistency while stirring
  3. drizzle the glaze across the top of the warm cinnamon buns and yum you have some gorgeous gooey cinnamon rolls!
  4. enjoy and eat!
