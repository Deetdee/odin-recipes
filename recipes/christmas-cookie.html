Christmas Cookies

Plate of decorated cookies ready for the holidays!

Christmas cookies are holiday tradition always on the shelves during Christmas times. This recipe will allow you to bake

and decorate your own and I guarantee that it will soon become a new holiday tradition for your family once you try it.

Ingredients

  • 3 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup margarine (butter), softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl; set aside.
  2. beat sugar and margarine in a large bowl with a hand mixer until smooth. Beat first egg into butter mixture. Beat second egg into mixture along with vanilla extract; add flour mixture and stir until dough is just combined. Wrap dough; chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  3. preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit (200 degrees celsius). Grease two cookie sheets.
  4. Roll out the dough on a clean floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut out shapes using cookie cutters; arrange them on the prepared cookie sheets. Gather scraps and repeat.
  5. Bake in batches in the preheated oven until edges are golden, about 6 to 8 minutes. Cool on the baking sheet briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
